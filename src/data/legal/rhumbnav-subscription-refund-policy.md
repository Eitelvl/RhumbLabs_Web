Last Update: 12/05/2026

Effective Date: Upon publication of the Application

This Subscription and Refund Policy ("Policy") governs the subscription plans, billing cycles, trial periods, cancellation procedures, and refund conditions applicable to the mobile application RhumbNav (the "Application"), operated by Rhumb Labs (the "Company").

This Policy forms an integral part of the Terms and Conditions of Use of the Application. For general terms governing the use of the Application, please refer to the Terms and Conditions.

## 1. Subscription Plans

The Application offers two access tiers:

**1.1 Free Plan** A limited version of the Application is available at no cost. The Company reserves the right to modify, restrict, or discontinue the features included in the Free Plan at any time without prior notice.

**1.2 Paid Plan** Full access to the Application requires a paid subscription. Subscription plans may be offered on a monthly or annual basis, as shown within the Application or the applicable app store at the time of purchase.

## 2. Free Trial

A 30-day free trial of the Paid Plan may be offered to new Users only and may be limited to one trial per person or device.

At the end of the trial period, the subscription will automatically renew at the applicable rate unless the User cancels before the trial expires.

Users are responsible for monitoring the end date of any free trial. Notifications may be provided by the applicable platform where available.

If the User cancels during the free trial period, access to Paid Plan features will continue until the end of the trial and will then revert to the Free Plan

## 3. Automatic Renewal

Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period unless cancelled by the User prior to renewal. By starting a subscription, the User expressly agrees to recurring billing until cancellation.

The Company or the relevant app platform will notify the User prior to the renewal of any subscription, in accordance with the policies of the applicable platform.

The User authorizes recurring charges according to the selected subscription plan.

## 4. Pricing

Pricing is subject to change. Current pricing details are provided within the Application and the applicable app store.

Users will be notified in advance of any price changes, which will take effect in the next billing cycle. Price changes will not affect the current billing period already paid. Prices may vary by country, currency, taxes, and platform fees.

## 5. Cancellation

Users may cancel their subscription at any time directly through Google Play or Apple App Store, depending on the platform used.

Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period. The User will retain access to the Paid Plan until the end of the period already paid for.

Upon cancellation, the User will retain access to the Free Plan.

RhumbNav does not manage cancellations directly. Cancellations must be processed through the platform where the subscription was purchased.

## 6. Purchases and Refunds

All purchases are final and non-refundable, except where required by applicable law or the policies of Google Play or Apple App Store.

RhumbNav does not process refunds directly. All refund requests are managed exclusively by Google LLC (Google Play) or Apple Inc. (App Store), according to their respective policies:
- Google Play refund policy: https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637
- Apple App Store refund policy: https://support.apple.com/en-us/111900

The approval or rejection of refund requests is determined solely by Google or Apple, as applicable

## 7. Payment Processing

All payment transactions are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing or Apple App Store. The Company does not collect, store, or process payment card information.

If a payment cannot be successfully processed, access to paid features may be suspended until payment is resolved.
